About Artemis Studio

My name is Juliana, I am a 26-year-old composer from Argentina, and the owner of Artemis Studio. I graduated as an Orchestral Conductor from the National University of La Plata, located in the city of La Plata, Buenos Aires. During my academic journey, I started to dive in the path of the composition and explored the intricacies of musical production. My creative endeavors are primarily focused on crafting music for audiovisual productions such as video games, podcasts, films, and more. My main focus lies in establishing a seamless connection between the visual and auditory realms, allowing me to translate visual narratives into the music I create. While I am capable of producing music in various styles to suit your project, my true expertise lies in epic medieval compositions.
